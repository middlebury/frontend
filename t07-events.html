What’s Your Mission?

I want to
  • help build a more just world.
  • be a catalyst for social change.
  • take on the world’s most important sustainability challenges.
  • bridge cultures through international education.
  • promote global prosperity.
  • make the world safe from violent extremism and weapons of mass destruction.
  • build understanding through language.
  • facilitate communication worldwide.
  • open global markets through technology.
  • teach English to students around the world.
